Hi! I'm Rosa.

I'm from Oak Park, IL, a village on Chicago’s west side known for its prairie-style architecture. Oak Park and River Forest High School’s parent association lovingly crowned the institution "Smoke Park and Reefer Forest".

My mother’s African-American family hails from the sticky bowels of Alabama, & my father’s from a sheep-laden suburb of Hannover, Germany, making me Afro-German.

I’m vegan. I love local honey, but stay away from root vegetables.

My jazz-piano-major college roommate and I ran a weekly underground bar in our dorm room featuring a selection of mixed drinks, seasonal beers, and live music.
